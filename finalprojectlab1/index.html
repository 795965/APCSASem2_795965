This is the first cat, we can name him Jerry

This is Jerry. Jerry is just a bit off. He can't tell the difference between his tongue and his eyes, but he is trying very hard. Jerry loves all animals and gets along with stuffed animals. He likes to take selfies. His favorite food is tuna.

This is the second cat, he is called Frank

This is Frank. Frank loves to play golf. He is a little bit fat but he loves his body. His family is trying to steer him away from chicken, but he just loves it so much. Frank used to get made fun of for not having any hair. Now he eats away his sorrows. Frank's inspiration is Sonia Sotomayor and one day he hopes to be a justice on the Supreme Court.

This is the third cat, her name is Sharon

This is Sharon. She thinks that she is secretly a human. She loves to stand and recently has learned how to walk on two feet. She loves dressing herself up, but no one can know about it. She loves to go get coffee with the other felines on her block. She has a crush on Frank, but he can never know about it. Her family would kill her if she ever got together with Frank.

This is the fourth cat, her name is Susan

Susan is suffering from a bout of depression after her boyfriend left her. Susan is naive and young. She is unaware of the perils of Katville. she is struggling to maintain self confidence. She just got her hair redone in order to feel more like herself. She hopes to make more girlfriends soon, but doesn't have her eye on anybody yet.

This is the fifth cat, his name is Bunny

Bunny is a master of disguise. Bunny can dress up as a rat, a mouse, and a bunny. He is the town musician and loves to entertain. He love to play with kids but lives alone in his house.

This is the sixth cat, his name is Hitller

This is Hitller he was exiled to KatVille from Catmany. He hates life and wants to go to the Ocean. The other cats in Katville try to make him feel more accepted. He hasn't done anything wrong, he is just mean to a lot of people.
